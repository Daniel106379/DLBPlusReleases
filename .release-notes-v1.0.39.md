## DLBPlus 1.0.39

### What's new
- **Continue Watching home reset:** Returning to Home now resets CW focused index, horizontal scroll, and hero together on Details/Search back, SideNav Home, app launch, and route changes
- **CW row-down navigation:** Moving down from Continue Watching to the next rail keeps the white focus ring visible and prevents focus jumping back to CW
- **Hero sync:** Home hero always matches the actually focused carousel item after reset and vertical navigation
- **My List hero logo restored:** Hero shows title logo when available; landscape cards remain logo-free

### Install / update
- In-place update from **v1.0.0** through **v1.0.38** is supported (same `dlbplus.jks` signing key).
- Primary asset: `app-universal-release.apk`
